After the collapse of central governments and the disintegration of national borders, the world stood at the threshold of a new era. Fragmented and weakened by decades of conflict, traditional governance systems were no longer capable of managing global movement and identity in a rapidly changing, interconnected world.

In their wake, corporations created a new structure for citizenship: fluid, temporary, and subscription-based. OmniPass became the world's first Citizenship-as-a-Service (CaaS) platform, allowing individuals to easily navigate across regions, subscribing to the rights and privileges they need for as long as they need them.

OmniPass marked the dawn of a new global identity—where nationality is no longer a birthright but a service. Citizens can now purchase access to territories, legal systems, and communities, all through a unified, digital platform. This system empowers global citizens to move freely, leveraging technology for a fluid global existence.

How it works

Step 1

Choose a Path

Select from one of four paths-Seeker, Resident, Denizen, or Nomad— according to your current needs.

Step 2

Get a Plan

After selecting a path, you'll be presented with a detailed description of the recommended plan, including benefits and subscription details

Step 3

Provide Verifiers

Enter the names of two trusted verifiers, either human or non-human, who can confirm your identity.

Step 4

Access Rights

Once verified, complete payment to unlock citizenship rights and services based on your plan. Manage, upgrade, or extend your rights as needed.
